Tennis for All

Tennis is a sport for a lifetime, and for all ages and abilities. No other sport delivers the wide variety of benefits that tennis does, whether you're just starting out or you've played for years. Learn about the sport's benefits, the basic rules, find a place to play–and, most importantly, have fun!

Youth Tennis

Tennis is easy to learn and tailored for all ages and abilities, giving kids a game that will help them build friendships and learn skills they'll use for life. The USTA has a range of programs designed for all kids looking for fun and a positive play experience. Our developmentally appropriate programs include individual and team play, wheelchair programs, and programs for those who need it most.

College Tennis

Tennis doesn't have to end after high school. Tennis is thriving on college campuses: on varsity teams, with the Tennis on Campus program, and through intramural opportunities. The USTA has resources available to help you pick the program that is right for you.

Adult Tennis

From competitive programs like USTA League and tournaments, to casual play options like social leagues and team-up events, adult tennis programs offer something for players of all skill levels aged 18 and over. Learn more about some of our signature programs.
